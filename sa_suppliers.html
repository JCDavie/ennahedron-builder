What to ask when phoning each supplier

For XLAM / MTT (Route 1)

  • "Can you cut compound bevels of 33.90° and 40.89° on the perimeters of CLT panels up to 6 m × 6 m, accurate to ±0.2°?"
  • "I'll supply an OBJ file with 9 panels (3 squares + 6 kites). Can you take that as input directly, or do you need it converted to your CAM format?"
  • "What's the lead time for one home (9 panels) versus a 5-home production run?"
  • "Per-m² rate for material + cutting setup fee, please. Do you offer a discount for production runs?"
  • "Can the panel include a 90 × 90 mm solid pine edge laminate around the perimeter, glued before the bevel cut?"

For Felder / Geerlings (Route 2)

  • "Quote for the K 740 with Power-Drive (electronic blade tilt)" — for Felder. "SCM Nova SI 400 with electronic tilt" for Geerlings.
  • "What's the angular repeatability of the blade-tilt mechanism? (Look for ≤0.1°.)"
  • "Bring me to a showroom — I want to make a test compound-bevel cut at 33.9° and measure the result with a digital gauge."
  • "Lead time, delivery, installation, training, finance options."

For Festool retailers (Route 3)

  • "Festool TS 75 EBQ-Plus-FS at the discounted price you've shown online — do you have it in stock today?"
  • "Two FS 3000/2 rails + the rail connector (FSV) and four guide rail clamps."
  • "Trade discount — many SA Festool retailers will trim 5–10 % on a kit purchase if asked."

My picks

  • If you want to build one home now: Phone XLAM in Cape Town and request a quote (+27 84 66 11 095). They have the best CNC for compound bevels in the country and locally-grown SA pine. If their quote is over ~R 250 k for a single home's panels, fall back to Route 3.
  • If you want to start a small fab business: Visit Felder in Centurion (+27 12 643 0515) and run a test cut on a K 740 with Power-Drive. R 350–500 k all-in.
  • If this is a one-off self-build under R 60 k of tooling: Walk into Toolcraft Plumstead (Cape Town) for the TS 75 + rails, and order the Wixey WR300 from Ubuy. You'll have the kit on the bench within two weeks.
