Notes on this catalog

  • Counts are exact. Squares, kites, L-edges, S-edges, bolts and pine totals are derived from the join structure of each preset and have no slack — they will match the on-site cut list once you choose L.
  • Foundation is heuristic. The "ground modules" figure assumes vertical stacks (square↔square via the bottom face) put one module above another, removing it from the ground footprint. Walking around the actual preset in the builder will let you confirm; correct manually if your real layout differs.
  • Costs scale with your L. Halving L drops material area by 4× and costs roughly with it; doubling L roughly quadruples them. The bevel angles (33.90° / 40.89°) do not change with L.
  • Same bolt scheme everywhere. The demountable M16 join described in the panel design doc is used at every panel-to-panel edge — exterior rim and interior partitions alike — so any home in this catalog can be unbolted and moved.
  • Glazing and doors not in shell $. Add ~$1,100/m² for triple-glazed wall panels and ~$5k per exterior door. A typical small home (3–5 modules) needs one door + ~10–20 m² of glazing.
